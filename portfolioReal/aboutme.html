Today, I am my family's very own computer technician because of all the skills I have acquired across the years. Moving forward in my education, I am planning on specializing my education and learning the intricacies and details involving the function of computers and electronics, studying computer science and hardware manufacturing. I hope to land myself a job at some sort of tech manufacturing company such as Nvidia or Intel, or maybe one day beginning a manufacturing business of my own. I believe that the tech industry, in its current state, could use more competition, especially in the form of processors and video card technologies, which I aim to solve.
